# 00 Mission Briefing

Your mission is to read a `STATIC_FIXTURE` signal and leave a clear local trail of what you observed.

You will move through:

- Control Room: the public Lab.
- Signal: the plotted `STATIC_FIXTURE` record.
- Logbook: your observation and hypothesis.
- Run: a local tracked activity.
- Artifact: saved records from the Run.
- Observatory: local static pages for inspection.
- Compare: a two-Run local comparison.

## Mission Boundary

This mission uses `STATIC_FIXTURE` data only.
It is read-only analysis and decision support.
It does not control hardware or provide hazardous operating procedures.
It does not fetch external fusion data.
It is not a standalone authority for safety-critical operation or reactor design decisions.
